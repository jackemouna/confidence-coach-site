Questions

Answered honestly

Is this therapy?

No. Confidence Coach is personal development, not therapy or medical care. The score is a personal progress indicator, not a diagnosis. If you're struggling, the app points you to real support, and we mean that.

How does the AI know my confidence level?

You answer six questions in your own words. The AI analyzes content and writing style: hedging, avoidance markers, agency language, and how you describe your inner critic. Scores are clamped and computed server side.

Can I lose points?

Yes. Avoid your challenge and your score dips a little. That honesty is what makes climbing feel real. One skipped day never erases a week of effort.

What happens to my data?

It stays yours. View and edit everything your coach knows, export it all as JSON, or delete your account and every trace from inside the app. See the Privacy Policy.

What if a challenge feels wrong?

Swap it with one tap and tell your coach why. Anything that feels unsafe is discarded and replaced with something safer. Challenges never require spending money and are always your choice.
