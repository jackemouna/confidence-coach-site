Privacy Policy

Confidence Coach · Last updated July 15, 2026

Confidence Coach is a personal development app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the controls you have. The short version: your coaching data belongs to you, we do not sell it, and you can export or delete everything from inside the app.

What we collect

  • Account: your email address and sign-in identifier.
  • Coaching content: your assessment answers, daily reflections, chat messages with the coach, your goal, and the coaching profile the AI builds from them.
  • Progress data: confidence scores, challenge results, streaks, and evidence items you save.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active subscription, via Apple. We never see your payment details.
  • Usage events: anonymous events such as "check-in started" or "plan generated". These never contain the content of your messages.

How AI processing works

Your reflections and answers are sent to our server, which uses OpenAI's API to generate your coaching content. We send only the minimum context needed: your coaching profile, recent history, and the current conversation. We request that the provider not retain responses for training. The numeric confidence score is always computed by our server, never invented by the AI.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (hosted on AWS in the United States), protected by row level security so only your account can read your rows. Coaching audio is generated on demand and stored privately with short-lived signed links. Photos you add to your Evidence Vault stay on your device.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal data.
  • We never put your message content into analytics.
  • We never use your coaching content for advertising.

Your controls

  • See what the coach knows: Profile → What My Coach Knows shows the AI's memory. You can edit or delete any item, or clear it all.
  • Export: Profile → Export Personal Data gives you a complete copy in JSON.
  • Delete: Profile → Delete Account permanently removes your chats, profile, scores, plans, evidence, audio, and account. Deleting your account does not cancel an Apple subscription; manage that in your device Settings.

These controls are available to every user, free or paid.

Safety

If messages indicate a safety risk, the app pauses normal coaching and shows crisis resources. Confidence Coach is not therapy, medical care, or an emergency service.

Age

Confidence Coach is intended for adults 18 and older.
